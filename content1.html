Techniques and uses
Technique Goal Process
Serve To start and maybe win a point. Ball is thrown in the air and hit on the way down.
Bump To recover the ball, start a play, basic pass Both wrists together, a fist inside of a palm
Set To prop up a ball to be spiked Finger tips push the ball into the air
Spike Score points Approaching and jumping a ball that has been set, then swinging the ball into the other side
Block Stopping the ball from crossing into your side of the court Jumping in front of the net with palms making a wall above the net
